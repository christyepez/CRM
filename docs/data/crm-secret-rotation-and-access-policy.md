# CRM Secret Rotation and Access Policy

Status: planned.

Future requirements:
- Secret access must be least-privilege.
- Rotation must be documented before non-production runtime activation.
- Rotation evidence must be captured during Sprint 3 P3/P4 planning.
- No developer-local secrets may be committed.
- No shared passwords across PortalCorporativo, Financiero and CRM.

Current P2 scope is contract-only and does not create real vaults or secrets.
