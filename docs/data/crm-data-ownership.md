# CRM Data Ownership

## CRM-owned future concepts

- Lead lifecycle.
- Account relationship context.
- Contact relationship context.
- Opportunity pipeline context.

## Shared boundaries

- Portal owns users, permissions, menu, audit, notification, configuration and gateway concerns.
- Financiero owns accounting, invoicing and tax workflows.
- Customer/account overlap must be resolved through contracts, not shared tables.

## Master-data guardrail

CRM must not become an accidental customer master-data source for Financiero until ownership is explicitly approved.
