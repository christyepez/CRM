# CRM Contacts Foundation

Sprint 1 P3 defines Contact behavior in foundation mode only.

## Rules

- Contact requires person name.
- Email is optional but validated when supplied.
- Phone is optional but validated when supplied.
- Role/title is conceptual metadata.
- Contact can be assigned to an account reference.
- Preferred contact method must match available contact data.

## Not production

- No contact CRUD screen.
- No persistence.
- No login, token storage or Portal permissions duplication.
