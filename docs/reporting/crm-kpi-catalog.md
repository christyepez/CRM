# CRM KPI Catalog

Foundation KPI catalog:

- LeadConversionRate
- QualifiedLeads
- OpportunitiesWon
- OpportunitiesLost
- PipelineValue
- AverageSalesCycleDays
- ActivitiesCompleted
- FollowUpsPending
- AccountsActive
- ContactsActive

All KPI definitions use `FoundationMock` and do not contain real customer, sales or financial data.
